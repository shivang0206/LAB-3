Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place two slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
  4. Add a slice of cheese and some tomato slices to each sandwich.
  5. Add a slice of cooked bacon on top of each sandwich.
  6. Top each sandwich with another slice of bread, butter side up.
  7. Cook the sandwiches until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
